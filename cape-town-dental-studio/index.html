FAQs

Do you accept medical aid?
Yes — we work with all major medical aids in South Africa including Discovery, Bonitas, Momentum, and GEMS. We'll handle the claims paperwork for you.
How do I book an appointment?
Easiest is WhatsApp or the booking form on this page — we'll confirm within an hour during business hours. You can also call us directly.
What if I have a dental emergency?
Call us immediately. We keep slots open daily for emergencies and will see you the same day whenever possible.
Do you see children?
Absolutely. Our team is experienced with anxious patients of all ages, and we have a calm, kid-friendly approach.
Is parking available?
Yes, free on-site parking at the practice.
